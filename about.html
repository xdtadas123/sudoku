Sudoku is a logic-based number puzzle in which the board is divided into rows, columns, and small squares. Each row, column, and square must contain all the numbers from 1 to 9 without repeating.

This version lets you choose different difficulty levels, tracks time, mistakes, and empty cells. The goal is to fill the board using logic rather than guessing.

Regularly solving Sudoku improves concentration, memory, and the ability to notice patterns. It is a calm yet engaging workout for the brain.
